Services

Here is what we offer for your team.

Natural Language Understanding

The application provides that users gives commands in English to chatbot. The chat bot understands commands and returns with appropriate actions.

Cubicl Integration

Our Virtual assistant applies commands which are received from users, to control the Cubicl. The system efficiently process the user intent. Some of them are creating, assigning, listing and deleting tasks.

Natural Language Processing

After retrieving the commands successfully, the chatbot responds with affirmative sentences. The assistant is able to greet and make daily speeches to users. In case of ambiguity, the assistant can ask for more information to help you reach your goal faster.
